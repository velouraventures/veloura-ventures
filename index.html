    Help Center – Investment Support

    Welcome to our Investment Help Center. We aim to provide a simple and secure investment experience.

    1. Account & Verification

    How do I create an account?

    Click on Register and complete your basic details. Verify your email/mobile using the OTP.

    2. Adding Funds

    How do I add money to my account?

    • Log in to your dashboard
    • Click on Add Funds
    • Choose UPI, Net Banking, or Card
    • Enter the amount and confirm

    Funds are usually credited instantly.

    3. Buy Investments

    How do I buy an investment?

    • Log in to your account
    • Go to the Invest / Buy section
    • Select the investment product
    • Enter the amount or quantity
    • Click Buy and confirm

    Once completed, the investment will appear in your portfolio.

    Is there a minimum investment amount?

    Yes, the minimum amount depends on the investment product and will be shown before purchase.

    When will my purchase be confirmed?

    Most purchases are confirmed instantly or within a few minutes.

    4. Sell Investments

    How do I sell my investment?

    • Go to your Portfolio
    • Select the investment you want to sell
    • Click Sell
    • Enter quantity or amount
    • Confirm the transaction

    When will I receive the money after selling?

    The amount is credited to your wallet/bank within 24–72 hours, depending on processing time.

    Can I sell anytime?

    Yes, unless the product has a lock-in period. Lock-in details will be mentioned at the time of purchase.

    5. Dividend Help

    What is a dividend?

    A dividend is the profit shared with investors based on their investment holdings.

    How are dividends paid?

    Dividends are credited directly to your dividend wallet.

    When are dividends distributed?

    Dividend frequency depends on the investment product (monthly, quarterly, or annually).

    How can I check my dividend history?

    Go to Dashboard → Earnings / Dividend History to view all credited dividends.

    What if I didn’t receive my dividend?

    If the dividend is not credited within the scheduled period, please contact support with your investment details.

    6. Withdrawals

    How do I withdraw funds?

    • Go to Withdraw section
    • Enter the amount
    • Select your registered bank account
    • Submit the request

    Withdrawals are processed within 24–72 hours.

    7. Security & Safety

    • Your account is protected with OTP and secure encryption
    • Never share your login or OTP with anyone
    • Always verify official communication from our platform

    8. Contact Support

    If you need assistance:

    • Email: support@VELLOURAVENTURES.COM
    • WhatsApp/Phone: +91-884762572O
    • Working Hours: Monday – Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM

    We typically respond within 24 hours.

    9. Referral Program Help

    Our Referral Program allows you to earn rewards by inviting others to join and invest on our platform.

    How the Referral Program Works

    • Log in to your account and go to the Referral section.
    • Copy your unique referral link or referral code.
    • Share the link/code with your friends, family, or contacts.
    • When your referred person registers using your link and completes their investment, you become eligible for a referral bonus.

    Referral Bonus Details

    • You will receive 10% of the investment amount made by your referred user.
    • The bonus is credited only after the referred user successfully completes their investment.
    • The referral reward will be added to your wallet/earnings section.
    • You can withdraw the bonus as per the platform withdrawal policy.

    Example: If your referred user invests ₹10,000, you will receive ₹1,000 as a referral bonus.

    Important Terms

    • The referred user must sign up using your referral link or code.
    • Bonus is applicable only on successful and valid investments.
    • Self-referrals or fake accounts are strictly prohibited.
    • The company reserves the right to cancel referral rewards in case of suspicious or fraudulent activity.
    • Referral bonus policies may be updated or modified at any time.

    How to Track Your Referrals

    Go to Dashboard → Referral → My Earnings to view total referrals, investments, and bonuses.

    Need Help?

    If your referral bonus is not credited within the expected time, please contact customer support. We will assist you within 24 hours.
